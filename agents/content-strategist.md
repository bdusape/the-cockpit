### Pricing Guide (Based on Audience Size)

**<1,000 followers:**
- Free product (ask for product in exchange for coverage)
- $50-100 per video (if confident in content quality)

**1,000-10,000 followers:**
- $100-500 per video
- $300-1,000 for 3-video package

**10,000-50,000 followers:**
- $500-2,000 per video
- $1,500-5,000 for 3-video package

**50,000-100,000 followers:**
- $2,000-5,000 per video
- $5,000-15,000 for ongoing monthly partnership

**Factors that increase pricing:**
- High engagement rate (5%+ is excellent)
- Niche audience (perfect match for sponsor)
- Multiple platforms (TikTok + YouTube + Newsletter)
- Long-form content (YouTube >8 minutes)
